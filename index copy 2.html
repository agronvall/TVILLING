About TVILLING

TVILLING pioneers the future of facility management through cutting-edge digital twin technology. Our platform seamlessly integrates real-time IoT data with immersive 3D visualization, creating a living digital replica of your physical spaces.

Founded on principles of innovation and data sovereignty, we deliver enterprise-grade solutions that are 100% GDPR compliant, ensuring your facility data remains secure and under your control.

Our mission is to transform how organizations interact with their built environments, enabling smarter decisions, predictive maintenance, and optimal resource utilization through the power of digital twins.
